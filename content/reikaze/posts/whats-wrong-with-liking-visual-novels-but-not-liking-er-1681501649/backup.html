I probably like VN's more than I really should. It's my favorite medium of storytelling, and I've experienced tons of great stories. I love it because it combines the best of a written medium with a Visual/Audio Medium, and the stories are great. Visual Novels are so great because they have near unlimited control of narrative, and because of this, I've seen some great narratives out there and I've enjoyed most of them. Visual Novels are just enjoyable for me, and I tend to gravitate to them because I know that i'll most likely have a good time with them... but eroge has always been a pain point.

I oppose eroge for the same reasons that many oppose fanservice in anime (which I dislike quite a bit as well). I love Visual Novels because of the narrative, and eroge is something that gets in the way of the narrative in the worst way possible, and that was pretty much the basis for the last third of my article, the one that actually talks about Eroge. It seems like though, that community directly opposes my viewpoint, and opposes my beliefs. Why can't I like something for the narrative, but dislike the aspects that get in the way of it? What's wrong with holding a viewpoint like this, and why does the community I tried so hard to promote immediately slam on me the second I state this?
